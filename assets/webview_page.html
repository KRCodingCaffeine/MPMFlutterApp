Social Security

  • National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP): While there isn't one single page for the entire NSAP, its components are often found within the Ministry of Rural Development's website. A good starting point is: https://rural.nic.in/ You'll need to search within this site for specific aspects of NSAP (like old age pensions, widow pensions, etc.)
  • Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY): https://jansuraksha.gov.in/
  • Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY): https://jansuraksha.gov.in/
  • Atal Pension Yojana (APY): https://pfrda.org.in/ (Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority's site)

Food and Nutrition

  • Public Distribution System (PDS): This is largely state-specific. You'll need to search for the PDS website of the particular state you're interested in. The national portal sometimes has links: https://consumeraffairs.nic.in/
  • Midday Meal Scheme: https://pmposhan.nic.in/
  • Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS): https://wcd.nic.in/icds (Ministry of Women and Child Development's page)
  • Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY): Information is usually found within the Department of Food & Public Distribution's site: https://dfpd.nic.in/

Housing

  • Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY): https://pmay.gov.in/

Education

  • Right to Education (RTE): Often managed at the state level. NCERT (National Council of Educational Research and Training) might have some information: https://ncert.nic.in/
  • Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA): Now part of Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan: https://samagra.education.gov.in/
  • Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA): Also part of Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (see above).

Health

  • Ayushman Bharat: https://ayushmanbharat.gov.in/
  • National Health Mission (NHM): https://nhm.gov.in/

Rural Development

  • Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA): https://nrega.nic.in/
  • Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY): https://ddugky.gov.in/
  • Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY): https://pmgsy.nic.in/

Other Schemes

  • Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY): https://pmjdy.gov.in/
  • Mudra Yojana: https://mudra.org.in/
  • Start-up India: https://www.startupindia.gov.in/
  • Skill India Mission: https://skillindia.gov.in/
